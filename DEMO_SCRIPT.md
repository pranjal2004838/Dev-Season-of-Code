# Shadow SaaS Detector - Judge Pitch and Video Script

## Core Framing

Judges do not need a longer product tour. They need to feel three things, fast:

1. This is a real problem that already hurts real teams.
2. Your product makes that pain visible immediately.
3. Your solution goes beyond detection and helps a company actually act.

That means the demo should not start with features. It should start with a person.

Use this person throughout the video:

**Emma, an IT manager at a growing company.**

She is not careless. She is overloaded. Employees keep adopting tools without approval. Finance sees software charges. Legal worries about compliance. Security has no visibility. Emma is blamed for all of it.

That is the emotional hook.

---

## The 20-Second Opening Pitch

Use this if you need a short live intro before the demo.

> Every fast-growing company has the same hidden problem: employees adopt SaaS tools on their own, and IT only finds out after money is lost or data is exposed.
>
> We built Shadow SaaS Detector to help an IT manager see every unauthorized app, understand the security and compliance risk, and take action in one place.

---

## The Strongest 45-Second Pitch

Use this for judges if you need one concise, memorable explanation.

> Meet Emma, an IT manager at a growing company. Finance sees unexplained software charges, legal is worried about compliance, and employees are connecting random SaaS tools to company data without approval.
>
> The problem is not just wasted budget. It is invisible risk.
>
> Shadow SaaS Detector helps Emma discover unauthorized SaaS from real signals like expenses, browser activity, and connected accounts, score the risk with AI, identify duplicate tools, and generate a remediation path before shadow IT turns into a breach.

---

## Recommended Demo Structure

Best runtime: 100 to 130 seconds.

Flow:

1. Human problem
2. Instant discovery
3. Risk and compliance insight
4. Action and savings
5. Clear close

Do not try to show every feature. Show the journey from confusion to control.

## 2-Minute Video Script

This is the main script to record.

### 0:00-0:15 - Hook

> Emma manages IT for a growing company. She keeps getting the same question from leadership: why are we paying for tools nobody approved, and who already has access to our company data?
>
> That is the shadow SaaS problem. It is budget leakage, security exposure, and compliance risk all at once.

**On screen**
- Open the app on the landing dashboard.
- Keep the opening view stable for a second so the judges can absorb the product name and positioning.

### 0:15-0:35 - Discovery

> With Shadow SaaS Detector, Emma connects the systems every company already has, like Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, or expense data.
>
> In seconds, the platform scans those signals and reveals the apps IT never approved but employees are already using.

**On screen**
- Click one of the connect options, ideally Google Workspace.
- Let the authentication and scanning animation play.
- Pause once the detected apps load.

### 0:35-0:55 - Make The Risk Feel Real

> Now this stops being a spreadsheet problem and becomes a business problem.
>
> Emma can immediately see which tools are high-risk, which departments are using them, what sensitive data may be exposed, and how much shadow spend is accumulating every month.

**On screen**
- Point to the total apps detected.
- Point to monthly shadow spend.
- Point to the high and critical risk count.
- Briefly hover over one alarming app card.

### 0:55-1:20 - AI Value

> Detection alone is not enough. Emma needs prioritization.
>
> In AI Insights, we turn a noisy list of apps into decisions. Our system explains why an app is risky, where compliance issues appear, and where overlapping tools can be consolidated.
>
> So instead of manually reviewing everything, Emma knows what needs urgent action first.

**On screen**
- Open AI Insights.
- Show Risk Assessment first.
- Then switch to Smart Consolidation.
- Then briefly open Compliance Audit.

### 1:20-1:40 - Action

> The most important part is that we do not stop at visibility. For each risky app, we generate a playbook to help IT respond: who is affected, what to revoke, what to migrate, and what to verify.
>
> That means Emma can move from discovery to remediation without leaving the platform.

**On screen**
- Go back to Dashboard.
- Open a Playbook on one high-risk app.
- Show the action flow or simulated revoke.

### 1:40-2:00 - Close

> Shadow SaaS Detector gives IT teams what they are missing today: visibility, prioritization, and action.
>
> It helps companies cut duplicate spend, reduce security exposure, and respond before shadow SaaS becomes a real incident.
>
> This is not just finding hidden software. It is giving control back to the people responsible for protecting the business.

**On screen**
- End on the strongest dashboard or AI screen.
- Hold the final frame for two seconds.

## What To Emphasize While Speaking

Repeat these ideas in different words throughout the demo:

- Unauthorized SaaS is not just wasted spend. It is hidden business risk.
- IT teams are accountable, but often blind.
- Your product surfaces the problem from real company signals.
- AI helps prioritize and explain, not just decorate the UI.
- The platform leads to action, not just analysis.

---

## What Judges Are Most Likely To Remember

If delivered well, judges should leave with this impression:

> This team picked a very real enterprise problem, made it instantly understandable, and built a product that goes from detection to remediation.

That is the winning narrative.

---

## Delivery Notes

- Speak slower than you think you need to. Judges need clarity more than speed.
- Pause for one second after each major visual change.
- Do not read numbers too aggressively unless they are visible on screen.
- Keep your cursor calm. Fast mouse movement weakens perceived polish.
- If a screen is busy, speak to only one thing on it.

---

## Lines To Avoid

Avoid saying things like:

- We built this because it is cool.
- We use AI to analyze everything.
- Here are all our features.
- This can help companies maybe save some money.

These sound generic and reduce urgency.

---

## Strong Final One-Liner

End with this if you want a sharper final sentence:

> Shadow SaaS Detector helps IT teams find what the company does not know it is running, before that hidden software becomes hidden risk.
